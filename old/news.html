19th

Our SXSW interviews couldn't have come to a more auspicious end, if we had scripted it. Our penultimate interviewee, Brian Phillips, neatly referred Chicago artists Nick and Nadine from Sonnenzimmer - who were our starting point! A beautiful coincidence that brought the project to a perfect circle.

Our interviews took us from visiting SXSW artists to Austin locals and back again. We found ourselves traversing across Austin city, seeing things and meeting people we would never have hoped to otherwise - not even in the chaos of SXSW. Their hospitality and friendliness was touching, and their unquestioned support, like that of our readers, made the whole project worthwhile.

To celebrate both the end of the project, its success and the pivotal role Sonnenzimmer came to play, we are giving away one of their screen printed beauties!

After months of toil, Gather & Fold is ready and will be launching tomorrow. G&F is an online design journal running on a referral system between creatives of all disciplines. This way, we hope to provide a snapshot of the contemporary design community - the relationships between emerging, outsider, unknown and celebrated designers - through their connections of mutual admiration.
