Gather and Fold

From 2011â€”2013, we interviewed 29 artists, designers, makers, photographers, illustrators and arcitects based in Melbourne. We published these interviews online as Gather and Fold.
This content wasn't curated; each interviewee chose who was to be interviewed next. They refered their friends, those they admired or collaborated with. Over time, the interviews traced the natural connections between disciplines, ages and experience levels: a cross-section of Melbourne's wider creative community.
